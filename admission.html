Academics

Overview of the School and Its Curriculum

Our school is a forward-thinking Nursery and Primary School strategically located in the heart of Lekki, Nigeria. Designed to meet the educational and developmental needs of young learners, the school provides a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children are guided to discover their abilities, develop confidence, and build a strong academic and moral foundation. With a commitment to excellence in early childhood and basic education, the school caters to crèche, nursery, and primary pupils, ensuring a smooth and supportive learning journey from infancy through the formative primary years.

At the core of our philosophy is the belief that every child is unique and deserves an education that nurtures the mind, body, and character. Our curriculum is carefully structured to promote academic competence, creativity, critical thinking, emotional intelligence, and social responsibility. By blending global best practices with Nigerian educational standards, the school prepares pupils not only for academic success but also for life beyond the classroom.

Crèche Programme: A Gentle Start to Learning

The school’s crèche programme is specially designed for infants and toddlers, providing a warm, secure, and loving environment that supports early development. At this stage, emphasis is placed on care, comfort, routine, and early stimulation. Experienced caregivers ensure that children receive individual attention, proper nutrition, and emotional support.

Activities at the crèche level focus on sensory exploration, motor skill development, early communication, and social interaction. Through play, music, storytelling, and guided movement, children begin to develop foundational skills that prepare them for the nursery stage. Safety, hygiene, and child wellbeing remain top priorities, giving parents peace of mind while their children take their first steps into structured learning.

Nursery Education: Building Strong Foundations

The nursery curriculum is thoughtfully crafted to support early childhood development and school readiness. At this level, learning is play-based, interactive, and engaging. Pupils are introduced to basic literacy and numeracy skills, phonics, early writing, number concepts, shapes, colours, and general knowledge.

The nursery programme also emphasizes social skills, communication, creativity, and independence. Children are encouraged to express themselves, work collaboratively, and develop confidence in their abilities. Activities such as storytelling, arts and crafts, singing, role-play, and outdoor play help pupils learn naturally while having fun.

Moral instruction, good manners, and positive values are introduced early, helping children develop respect, empathy, and self-discipline.

Primary School Curriculum: Academic Excellence and Holistic Growth

The primary school curriculum is comprehensive, balanced, and aligned with national educational standards while incorporating innovative teaching methods. Core subjects include:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Basic Science and Technology
  • Social Studies
  • Civic Education
  • Computer Studies / ICT
  • Creative Arts
  • Physical and Health Education

Lessons are delivered using interactive, learner-centred approaches that encourage curiosity, problem-solving, and independent thinking. Teachers use modern instructional tools, practical demonstrations, group work, and continuous assessment to ensure pupils fully understand concepts and can apply their knowledge confidently.

Beyond academics, the school places strong emphasis on character development, leadership skills, and moral values, ensuring pupils grow into responsible and confident individuals.

Innovative Learning Through Technology

Recognizing the importance of technology in today’s world, the school integrates technology-driven learning into its curriculum. Pupils are introduced to basic computer skills, coding, and digital literacy from an early age. These programmes help children develop logical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving abilities.

Through age-appropriate tools and activities, pupils learn how technology works and how it can be used responsibly and creatively. This early exposure prepares them for future academic pursuits and equips them with essential 21st-century skills.

Extracurricular Activities: Learning Beyond the Classroom

The school offers a rich variety of extracurricular activities designed to support physical development, creativity, innovation, and talent discovery. These activities complement academic learning and help pupils explore their interests in a well-rounded manner.

Some of the extracurricular activities offered include:

  • Coding and Programming
  • Robotics and STEM Activities
  • Swimming Lessons
  • Ballet and Creative Dance
  • Music (Vocal and Instrumental Training)
  • Drama and Role Play
  • Arts and Crafts
  • Sports and Physical Fitness Activities
  • Debate and Public Speaking (age-appropriate)
  • Clubs and Group Projects

These activities help pupils build confidence, teamwork, discipline, and creativity while also promoting physical fitness and emotional wellbeing.
